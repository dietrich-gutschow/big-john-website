Meet Big John

Born in a deep fryer and raised on a strict diet of MSG and mystery meat, Big John is the culinary antihero no one asked for; but everyone fears. Big John runs his takeaway like a fever dream that never ends. Hygiene inspectors have tried. Yelp reviewers have cried. But Big John? He just keeps cooking.
Came for the food, stayed because the door handle is stuck.
