Meet Big John

Born in a deep fryer and raised by fortune cookies, Big John is the mythical beast of takeaway cuisine. He once battered a microwave and called it Kung Pow Technology. Local health inspectors have tried to shut him down, but Big John simply deep-fries the paperwork. Rumor has it if you order the "Chef's Mystery Box", you may transcend space and taste.

Come for the noodles, stay because the door handle is stuck. Welcome to Big John's.
